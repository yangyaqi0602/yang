Gray, Painting, 100×180×60 cm, 2024, Calico and Mixed Paint, Kieken Centrale, Antwerp, BE

Gray combines painting and poetry to express the profound pain caused by war. The torn canvas and the stark tension of the words reveal the brutal truth hidden beneath a calm façade, urging people to confront silent fear and the struggles of humanity.
