Three broccoli stories, Drapes, 60×90cm, 2022, Prints on Fabric, HetHem, Zaandam, NL

Three Broccoli Stories explores love‘s stages—meeting, being together, and parting—through three intricate textile panels. Using broccoli‘s coded love gesture and multilingual phrases, the work creates a secret visual language, inviting viewers to interpret love‘s subtle mysteries through their own experiences.
